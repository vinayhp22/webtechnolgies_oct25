Margin

The space outside the border of an element wrt other element.

Adds external space around the element.

Can collapse vertically & horizontally

Can be uniform or Individual

Properties

For Uniform margin: margin

For Individual margin: margin-top, margin-buttom,margin-left,margin-right

Uniform Box - 50px margin

Individual Box - top - 10px, btm - 20px, l-30px, r-40px

Vertical-horizontal margin: vertical: 20px(t & b), horizontally: 40px(l & r)
