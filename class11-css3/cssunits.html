A paragraph is a distinct section of writing that focuses on a single idea or topic, consisting of one or more sentences. It is used to organize longer texts, making them easier for the reader to follow. Key components include a topic sentence, which introduces the main idea, followed by supporting sentences that provide details, and a concluding sentence to wrap up the thought.
